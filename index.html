Welcome

Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT) is an initiative by Saylani Welfare International Trust to provide free technical and vocational IT training to youth across Pakistan. SMIT aims to equip students with practical skills that enable them to pursue freelancing, local jobs, and entrepreneurship in the technology sector.

Why SMIT ?

SMIT offers no-fee courses, practical hands-on training, and career guidance. The program is designed to be accessible to students from diverse backgrounds so they can gain marketable IT skills without financial burden.
