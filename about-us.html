About Us

Background

Saylani Welfare International Trust was founded in 1999 and runs many welfare programs. SMIT (Saylani Mass IT Training) launched in 2013 with the goal of providing free IT and vocational training to young people so they can access employment and freelancing opportunities.

Mission

The mission of SMIT is to empower youth with practical, industry-relevant IT skills while removing financial barriers to technical education.

Approach

SMIT combines classroom instruction, hands-on labs, project work, and basic career guidance. Trainings are typically delivered at SMIT centers and may include short assessments or an entry check to place students in appropriate classes.

Who can apply?

SMIT typically welcomes students who have completed basic schooling (matric/intermediate or equivalent). Specific eligibility details and entry procedures vary by center and course; contact the nearly your area SMIT center for exact requirements.
